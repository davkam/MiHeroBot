	MiHeroBot is a Discord RPG bot created for entertainment and educational purposes.

!STATS
	LEVEL:    Represents the average stat of a hero, weapon or monster. Higher level means higher average in
		      attack, defense and health. 
		      (Max Level: 100) TBD
	ATTACK:   Is your offensive stat and determines the outgoing strike, higher attack means higher strike damage
			  and higher chance for critical strikes. 
		      (Max Attack: 500) TBD
	DEFENSE:  Is your defensive stat and determines the incoming strike, higher defense means higher damage absorption
	          and higher chance for critical strike negation. 
		      (Max Defense: 500) TBD
	HEALTH:   Is your health pool and determines how much incoming strike damage one can take before losing. 
		      (Max Health: 10000) TBD
	XP STORE: Is your storage of experience gained that can be used on any skill of your choosing. More info in !XP.
